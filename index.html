What We Do

Billings comprises of 3 main features. Payment, Loan, and Crowd Funding

Payment

QR Payment

This is the main function of the app. You can easily make payments to any merchant accepting Bilings Pay. Simply swipe open the app, scan QR of seller and confirm payments. No need to get change even for the smallest item.

Direct Transfer

A subsidiary to the main feature. You make mobile transfers to other Billings members using their phone numbers.

Loans

P2P Loans / Lending

We connect lenders and borrowers. Billings helps Lenders offer their funds, and borrowers borrow the funds through our platform. With this lenders money work for them with easy and our borrowers have quick access to funds which helps meet urgent financial needs needs with flexible loan repayment plan.

Crowdfunding / Fundraising

Crowdfunding

Like the saying goes "If you want to go far you go alone, but if you want to go further we go together" at Billings we understand the power of unity; that's why we’ve provided our users with a crowd funding platform, where we can all fund our various projects and campaigns be it educational, business, charity and so on.
